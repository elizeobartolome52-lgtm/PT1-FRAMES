TAPSILOG

  1. INGREDIENTS:
    1 kg. lean beef, sliced thinly
3 -4 tbsp cooking oil
8 cloves garlic, mashed
2 pcs Knorr Beef Cubes
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 -2 tbsp vinegar
Rice
Garlic
Egg
tomatoes
onions

    2. STEPS

  2. Make a marinade for your tapa by combining the oil, garlic, Knorr Beef Cubes, brown sugar and vinegar in a bowl.
    Mix this well until the ingredients are dissolved and free of lumps. Gently place the beef slices in and just leave
    it in the chiller to marinate overnight.
  3. The following day, drain the beef and let it dry because you don’t want any of those excess liquid when you fry the tapa.
  4. Fry the tapa in medium high heat with a little bit of oil. And that’s about it! Finally, this dish is done!
  5. Start frying your rice with garlic for around 15 minutes and cook your egg with tomatoes and onions.
  6. With a serving of garlic rice and egg or salted egg mixed with tomatoes and onions, you simply will want to
    have this any day any time of the week. Tapsilog is home!
